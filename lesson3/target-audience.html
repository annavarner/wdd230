Audience

The target audience for this website is a small business owner in the Massillon, Ohio area who is looking for opportunies to advertise, grow, and promote their business. They will also be interested in collaborating with other small business owners in the area to plan and carry out community events and activities. Other visitors will include members of the community or visitors who want information on local businesses and events.

Persona # 1

Robert is the owner of Bob's Pizza, a popular restaurant in downtown Massillon that was established by his father in 1980. He is 53 years old, married with two sons - one studying business in college and the other is a senior in High School. His wife and sons help at the restaurant and he hopes one of his sons will take over the business one day. Robert is an active member of the chamber of commerce and frequently helps to plan and sponsor community events. He uses the website to keep up with community events and find out about new small businesses in the area that he can collaborate with to continue to grow his business.


Persona # 2

Diana is a 44 year old mother from Cincinnati. Her son Julian is the starting quarterback for his High School football team and her daughter Lily plays Tuba in the marching band. They're planning an weekend trip to Massillon for the State Championship Football game next week. She checks out the chamber of commerce website to research local businesses and activities to do while in the area.


Scenarios:

  • Who won the High School Football game last night? What was the score?
  • Which restaurants are open for lunch in Downtown?
  • When is the annual Fall Festival?
  • How do I join the Chamber of Commerce and what are the benefits to doing so?
  • Which roads will be closed off during the Christmas parade?
  • What is the weather forecast for today?
