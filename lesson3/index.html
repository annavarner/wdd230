Purpose:

The purpose of this website will help to promote and protect the interests of small businesses in Massillon, a small city of about 32,500 located in Northeast Ohio. It will provide local weather, news, High School sports scores, and information about local businesses and upcoming events. Massillon has a lot to offer both to residents and visitors and this website will provide the resources to help small businesses in our area to grow and succeed, and for our residents to thrive.
