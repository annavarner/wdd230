About Carlsbad, California

Located about 35 miles north of San Diego, Carlsbad is known for having nearly perfect weather all year round! Looking for something to do? Carlsbad has beautiful clean uncrowded sandy beaches perfect for swimming, surfing, and sunbathing. The three lagoons, which span more than 1000 acres are ideal for fishing, bird-watching, hiking, and water recreation. In town, stroll down the picturesque streets and explore the many antique stores, sidewalk cafes, and boutique shops. And while you're there, visit us at our storefront market for the best high quality organic produce!

Visit our market!

Our storefront market at 123 Beach Blvd has been serving the Carlsbad area since 2015. Come on in for the widest selection of locally-grown organic produce and fresh made-to-order specialty drinks. Each of our suppliers are carefully selected to ensure they meet our high standards for quality, freshness, and sustainability.
